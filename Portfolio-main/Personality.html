My Personality and Academic Life

One of my strongest traits is Prospecting, which means I am naturally flexible, curious, and adaptable. I enjoy exploring different learning methods instead of following strict routines. This allows me to stay open to new ideas and adjust easily to academic challenges.

My personality is a mix of both shy and bold, depending on the environment or the people around me. While I may be quiet at first, I become confident and expressive once I feel comfortable.

How My Interests Shape My Learning

I enjoy watching movies, series, and anime, and I also love reading books—though not romance ones. These interests fuel my imagination and creativity, shaping how I view the world and express myself.

I don’t really have a specific role model, but I often find inspiration in the traits of characters or people I admire—like strength, loyalty, and perseverance. My favorite animal is the wolf, as I see them as symbols of independence, intelligence, and deep connection with their pack—qualities I strive to embody in my own life.

However, my tendency to keep options open also means I sometimes work best under pressure. I often approach tasks with spontaneity, which helps me come up with creative solutions even at the last minute.

My Inspiration

"Don't lose yourself" – Roronoa Zoro

What inspires me most is the idea of progress. I believe that growth doesn’t happen overnight, but through small steps and persistence. I’m inspired by my own journey — by how far I’ve come, the lessons I’ve learned, and the strength I’ve built along the way.
