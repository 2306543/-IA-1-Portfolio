Website Pages & Intent

  • Home: Gives an introduction to the portfolio and myself.
  • Professional: Displays my resume (downloadable and readable) and an overview of my intended career path.
  • Personality: Highlights my hobbies, inspirations, and a personality review.
  • Personal Development: Reviews Twelve Pillars (Ch. 4–7) and outlines lessons learned. It also includes my degree details and skills gained at UTECH.
  • Storyboard: The current page, which shows my website’s layout and structure.
  • References: Lists sources used throughout the portfolio.

Website Theme

The theme of my website is modern, sleek, and professional, using shades of blue and teal to create a smooth, calm gradient. The design aims to provide a visually engaging and user-friendly experience.

To add uniqueness and dimension, circular design elements were incorporated throughout the pages.

Intended Website Features

  • Gallery — Displays project visuals.
  • Embedded Videos — Showcasing inspirations.
  • Embedded Audio — Bringing my project to life.
  • Downloadable Resume — For easy access to my CV.
  • Navigation Bar — Smooth page transitions.
  • Hyperlinks — Simplifies internal and external navigation.
